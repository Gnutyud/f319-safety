F319 - Child Safety Standards

F319 is committed to protecting children and ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child safety.

Our Commitments

  • Strictly prohibit and actively prevent all content related to Child Sexual Abuse Exploitation/Material (CSAE/CSAM).
  • Provide reporting mechanisms for users to flag abusive content.
  • Review and remove reported content promptly.
  • Report relevant cases to authorities when required.

Report Concerns

Users can report concerns directly in the app or by email: info.bongmedia@gmail.com.
